4. How do I deliver multiple copies of the same magazine to the same address?

Ordering more than one subscription of a specific magazine to the same address on our website will result in extending the length of your subscription. You will NOT get 2 copies of the magazine. If you want to receive multiple copies of a magazine at the same address, please contact us and a Customer Service representative can help.

Automatic Renewal allows you to continue to receive your magazine subscriptions without the hassle of dealing with renewal notices and payments. Before each magazine renewal term you'll receive an email notice reminding you of your upcoming renewal. At that time you can choose not to renew your magazine. Or, as always, you can log in to my account or contact us

10. How long will it take to receive my order?

Our delivery dates are based on multiple factors, including the type of magazine you ordered and the publisher of the magazine.

Publishers generally ask that you allow:
– 4-6 weeks on weekly magazines
– 6-8 weeks on monthly magazines
– 8-10 weeks on seasonal or bi-monthly magazines
– Up to 12 weeks on quarterly or less-frequent magazines

However, you can anticipate faster delivery on many of our top-selling magazines. If you have not received your first order within the estimated delivery time stated above, please contact us or call Customer Service toll-free at 888-616-7679.
