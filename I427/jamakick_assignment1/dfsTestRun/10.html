Announcement 1 URGENT NOTIFICATION: Beware of fraudulent support requests.
Announcement:URGENT NOTIFICATION: Beware of fraudulent support requests.
  • Show Details
  • Hide Details
by Jeff A Wright (March 10, 2015)

We have received a number of reports where some members of this community are posing as Adobe Employees by offering to help troubleshoot issues personally using technologies such as Skype as a means to gain access to your computer and potentially your personal information.  All Adobe employees are required to have a red ‘STAFF’ below their profile picture, as well as an Adobe logo after their username. You can also see both the 'STAFF' badge and Adobe logo if you mouse-over their profile picture.

Announcement 2 Temporary Activation Expired error when opening CS3
Announcement:Temporary Activation Expired error when opening CS3
  • Show Details
  • Hide Details
by Jeff A Wright (August 31, 2017)

After reinstalling Creative Suite 3 you may receive the error, “Temporary Activation Expired.”  This is due to cached licensing data stored on the computer.  Complete details on how to resolve this error, and successfully install Creative Suite 3, can be found at https://helpx.adobe.com/x-productkb/global/temporary-activation-expired.html.
