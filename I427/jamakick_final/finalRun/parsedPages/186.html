8 Easy and Impressive Christmas Party Appetizers

By Vicky McDonald

  • Facebook
  • Twitter
  • Google+
  • Pinterest
  • StumbleUpon

If you're planning on hosting a Christmas party these showstopping recipes will make a lasting impression on your guests.

1. Olive Puffs

"These puff pastry-wrapped olives are our family's favorite holiday appetizer," says Syd. "We make them with a variety of olives -- pimento stuffed green, plump kalamata, Provencal -- just make sure they are pitted. We like to use strongly flavored olives; the flavor of canned ripe olives disappears in the puff pastry."

2. Nutella(R) Pastry Christmas Tree

This Nutella and puff pastry masterpiece will be a hit with everyone at the party -- adults and kids alike. Don't be intimidated by puff pastry. Working with puff pastry is easier than you think.

3. Pine Cone Cheese Ball

Make your cheese ball a little more festive with some fresh rosemary and almonds.

Photo by jillianlaura

4. Spinach and Ricotta Puff Pastry Christmas Tree

See how to turn delicious spinach and ricotta stuffed puffed pastries into a Christmas tree decorated with ornaments of sliced cherry tomatoes. It's a cinch to put together, and makes an awesome centerpiece for your Christmas party.

5. Christmas Tree Appetizers

Get creative with some guacamole dip, pita bread, red peppers, and pretzels.

Photo by barbara

6. Bacon Gorgonzola Pull Aparts

This divine pull-apart bread looks like a jolly Christmas wreath when adorned with a sprig of rosemary and some dried cranberries. This recipe is made with a can of biscuit dough and can be whipped up in less than an hour.

7. Parmesan Puff Pastry Stars

Just two ingredients and some star-shaped cookie cutters are all you need to create these cute, cheesy bites.

Photo by lacucinadinadia

8. Vegetable Christmas Tree with Broccoli

Instead of serving a regular plate of crudités, assemble your vegetables into a Christmas tree like this one.

Photo by barbara
