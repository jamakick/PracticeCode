Many campuses
and online

Indiana University has locations around the state, including core campuses in Bloomington and Indianapolis. You can also earn an IU degree online.

Which option is right for you?

IU Bloomington

A top-15 school for undergraduate teaching according to U.S. News & World Report, IU Bloomington is one of IU’s core campuses. It offers more than 550 academic programs and Big Ten athletics on a historic campus.

Explore IU Bloomington

IUPUI

A core campus in Indianapolis, IUPUI offers a unique blend of Indiana University and Purdue University to students in a community that embraces diversity and personal development. Year in and year out, U.S. News & World Report recognizes IUPUI for first-year experiences that help freshmen succeed.

Explore IUPUI

IUPUC

At Indiana University–Purdue University Columbus (IUPUC) you can earn an undergraduate or graduate degree from two globally recognized Big Ten schools: Indiana University and Purdue University. Degree programs include business, communication studies, mechanical engineering, and more.

Explore IUPUC

IU East

IU East is the premier four-year and master’s public institution in eastern Indiana and western Ohio. Offering more than 60 academic programs, it has a traditional campus in Richmond and online degree completion programs.

Explore IU East

IU Fort Wayne

Indiana University Fort Wayne focuses its academic mission and strengths on health science. Degree programs are offered in dentistry, medical imaging and medicine, nursing, and social work.

Explore IU Fort Wayne

IU Kokomo

IU Kokomo offers a wide variety of relevant degree programs, plus internships, international travel, high-tech classrooms, athletics, and a vibrant campus life. It’s all designed to prepare you for a lifetime of success.

Explore IU Kokomo

IU Northwest

Located just outside Chicago in northwest Indiana, IU Northwest offers more than 70 undergraduate degrees and more than 20 graduate degrees and certificates. It makes education personal, affordable, and life-changing.

Explore IU Northwest

IU South Bend

IU South Bend will help you fulfill your potential. It offers a classic college experience—including more than 100 degree programs, NAIA athletics, and student housing—in north central Indiana.

Explore IU South Bend

IU Southeast

Located just outside of Louisville, Kentucky, IU Southeast offers the area’s most affordable four-year degree. Seven Kentucky counties qualify for in-state tuition, and the campus has more than 100 degree programs and concentrations.

Explore IU Southeast

IU Online

Nationally ranked IU Online has 124 online academic programs and hundreds of online classes offered by various IU campuses. You get the quality you expect from IU, the convenience you demand, and the support you need—all at an affordable cost.

Explore IU Online
