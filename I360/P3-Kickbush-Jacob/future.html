Our Future

Our company's future is expanding from only doing scary tricks and Halloween to spreading Christmas cheer and making gifts for everyone. Our glorious leader Jack has decided he is tired of only ever scaring people, and wants the opportunity to make them happy as well. The following picture is where our new facility will be built, Christmas Town! With the help of good old Saint Nick, a.k.a Santa, we hope to build our own toy workshop and help Santa provide joy to all the children of the world! Maybe he can even join us for a Halloween show a time or two as well.
