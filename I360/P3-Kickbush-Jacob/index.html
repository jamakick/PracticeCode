About Us

We are based in Halloween Town, the place to be for all things Halloween. As you can see we live, breathe, and even dream Halloween in our fair town. Here we cook up our different scary shows and tricks, spearheaded by our very own CEO, Jack Skellington. If you want to know what we are all about, come see one of our shows performed live in the town square. We can promise it will make you scream!

Jack Skellington

This is the face of our company, the one and only Jack Skellington. He is the prince of fright, and lives up to his title admirably.
